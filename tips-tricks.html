Tips and Tricks for Genius

This list of suggestion comes from the pdf manual provided with the game.

  • For the beginning of the game is important to start mining coal and build enough worker cottages in order to develop the basis for a production cycle.
  • From the start and throughout the game is also recommended to keep the worker positions and population at equal levels.
  • Separating the factories from the worker communities increases their living standards (allowing higher upgrades of their houses).
  • Avoid fires building a fire department as soon as possible. But take in account that the building takes plenty of workers and maintenance costs.
  • Factories of any kind are of positive influence over your account balance. It is therefore of a great advantage to build many and make sure they have enough workers and raw materials to work at maximum efficiency.
  • Big investments have to be well calculated. The use of statistics is an absolute must. Keep in mind that early expenses, and for example loan payments, always come at the end of the year.
  • Strikes can lead to a partial stop in production, it is recommended to pay attention to international norms for worker's salaries.
  • Use the building status windows to identify any eventual problem there might be.
