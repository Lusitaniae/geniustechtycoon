Frequenty Asqued Questions. About the game content:
  • In Level 1 I need to build a railroad. How do I do complete the level?
    Build the rails from the station up to the end of the map, the black area.
  • May I build upon the trees?
    Trees and stones can be removed with the demolition tool.
  • I couldn't complete the quiz after inserting the solution, why?
    Have you pressed after entering the "Enter" key?
    In addition, you must also make sure that you do not have rounding errors in your solution.
  • Is it possible to print out the papers and letters, or to save?
    Yes, the newspapers and letters are available as image files in the directory "data\smart\game_resource\picture" of the game. Usually on "C:\Program Files\Oxford University\physics genius\".
  • When the game is over?
    Once you've seen the video with the final flight of the rocket into space, no more new tasks follow.
    However, you can continue playing after that as long as you want.
  • My goods production is still unprofitable. Why? What can I do about it?
    If you have too many factories which manufacture certain goods, creates an excess supply of these goods and thus prices fall so far that the production is unprofitable. Then you may need to demolish a few of them, after a while it's recommended to demolish all the bicycle factories but the first one (which is impossible to).
  • When a task I will not be the right solution. Is there a pattern for solving the tasks?
    Yes, check our Walkgrough guide.
  • I'm absolutely stucked with losses. Are there any cheats?
    Yes, you can type "cheat" anywhere (you don't need a type box) and do the following:
    Press "Ctrl M" for 100.000 in cash;
    Press "Ctrl +/-" to speed up or down the time.
    By right-clicking on the light bulb, a window opens for the solution of a problem.
    Note, however, that no scores at the cheat mode can be entered into the online highscore! You can "cheat" type it again to exit the cheat mode again.
  • Will there be a continuation of Genius Series?
    Unfortunately, no. After the three parts Genius physics, biology and politics, the game series is completed.

Questions regarding gameplay
If you are not sure which button clicks or do certain things can be done, maybe you'll find here the right answer.
  • How do you rotate buildings so that they fit into a gap?
    Buildings can be turned using the space bar.
  • How do I get the "free mode" and what is it anyway?
    In contrast to the "career mode", where you play through all levels by the order, in free mode you play whatever levels you choose to. This is only possible after you have unlocked these levels in Career Mode.
