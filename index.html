Genius:The Tech Tycoon

Genius Tech Tycoon is a business simulation game for pc but unlike the others it also envolves an education side. You start playing in 1850 where you're given a single bicycle factory, from here you're going to expand your production to locomotives, optic devices, motorcycles, automobiles, trucks, airships, airplanes and rockets.

But evolution is only possible if you answer the constant quizzes envolving Physics questions and laws where you'll need to order the planets of our solar system, calculate the energy efficiency of a locomotive or select the best thermal insulator material, for instance, in order to keep profiting on this competetive environment.

You're also responsible for building enough habitation for your workers, increase their life standards through schools, canteens and so on, having enough mines supplying you factories with raw materials and provide water and electricity for increased productivity.
